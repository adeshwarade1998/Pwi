BRAND STYLE:

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headings: bold, playful font (like Poppins, Nunito, or Baloo)
- Body: clean and readable
- Large font sizes for readability

"Make your child a confident reader with fun phonics learning"

2. ABOUT PAGE:

3. COURSE PAGE:

4. RESULTS / TESTIMONIALS:

6. CONTACT:

ANIMATIONS (VERY IMPORTANT):
